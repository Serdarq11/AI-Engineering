structured vs unstructured data

structured data: is organized into rows and columns

unstructured data: text files, images, videos and audio files lacks a defined structured
and cannot be organized into rows and columns. (%80 - %90)

** AI turns unstructered data into valuable insights and analyze them.

** AI can recognize patterns by examine pixels of an image, video etc. and then turns them into binary form.

Collecting and Preparing Data for AI Modeling

labeled data: recognizing something among lots of pictures, text, comments in order to know 
what is something or what is not.

unlabeled data: feeding AI with lots of data and provide it to recognize them by its own.

metadata: is the data that describes other data (author, date created, usage, file size etc.)
