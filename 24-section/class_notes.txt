The Bag of Words Model: counts which words appear in which documents

- from sklearn.feature_extraction.text import countVectorizer: transforms a collection of text documents 
into a matrix of token counts. In other words:
It breaks the text into words (tokens) and counts how often each word appears. This gives us 
the numerical represantation we need to apply machine learning techniques to text data

- The bag of words method only counts words 



Term Frequency Inverse Document Frequency (TF IDF): keeps more of the context behind each word compared to bag of words 
term frequency: how many times a word appears in a single document 
inverse document frequency: how many times a word appears in the entire collection of documents

-common words: lower score
